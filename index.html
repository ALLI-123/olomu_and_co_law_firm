About Our Firm

Trusted Legal Experts Since 2023

Olomu & Co Chambers was founded by Barrister Olomu with a vision to provide exceptional legal services rooted in integrity, professionalism, and deep understanding of Nigerian law.

Our team of seasoned attorneys specializes in corporate law, commercial litigation, real estate, intellectual property, and family law. We pride ourselves on delivering tailored solutions that protect our clients' interests while achieving their strategic objectives.

Based in Badagry, Lagos, we serve clients across Nigeria and internationally, combining local expertise with global perspectives.
